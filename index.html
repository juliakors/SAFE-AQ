About us

Hi! We are Noosphere Engineering School Team. Our project is Distributed system for Air Quality monitoring for SMART city based on IoT. Development of software-hardware system of Air Quality estimation based on satellite and in-situ data in concept of IoT. The system will be proceed in cloud environment, the data will be available via web and mobile application in 24/7 mode. WE PROPOSE A PROJECT TO REDUCE THE NEGATIVE CONSEQUENCES OF THE AIR POLLUTION PROBLEM.

Product features

Cheap sensors

Grids of in-situ sensors over cities, their interaction based on IoT concept

Data Fusion

Harmonization and up-scaling of satellite & in-situ data using self-developed algorithm

Air Quality Maps

Calculating Air Quality Index (AQI) according to European Environmental Agency

Web & Mobile App

Real-time data visualization using geoportal and mobile application

How to use it

Choose

There are several types of Air Quality maps: general for Ukraine (AMS AQI), detail for Kyiv (Kyiv AQ MAP) and Rivne. You can also view satellite data of Sentinel-5P

View

By clicking on corresponding map You can view the state of Air Quality in any place of Ukraine for all availble dates

Estimate

Our data corresponds to European Standards (European Environmental Agency, EEA)

Discover

Explore the Air Quality changes during available timeliness via graphic plots
